## Pro Tips for Space Tourists

**Essential Equipment:**
- Extra fuel reserves
- Oxygen tanks and EVA suits
- Rover for surface sites
- Scanning equipment
- Light sources for dark locations

**Safety First:**
- Check local emergency channels before landing
- Many locations are in lawless or dangerous areas
- Bring friends for high-danger zones
- Always have an escape route planned

**Photography Tips:**
- **Best Times:** Sunrise/sunset for atmospheric shots
- **Angles:** Aerial views work best for geological formations
- **Safety:** Never put ship in dangerous positions for shots

**Navigation Advice:**
- Use coordinates as starting points, be prepared to explore
- Many locations require rover or EVA exploration
- Keep backup navigation data
- Watch for environmental hazards
