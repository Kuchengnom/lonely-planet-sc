## Benny Henge

**System:** Stanton
**Location:** Daymar
**Coordinates:** `-275.6, 24.4, 762.5`

### Description
Ancient stone circle structure, likely an easter egg referencing Stonehenge. A mysterious archaeological site with standing stones arranged in a circle pattern.

### How to Reach
Fly to Daymar, look near the coordinates. Location is on the surface.

### What Makes It Special
Mysterious ancient ruins, very atmospheric, great for photos

### Lore & Background
Origin unknown, possibly ancient alien civilization or precursor to current species

### Visitor Tips
Best viewed at sunrise/sunset. Bring a rover for easier exploration.

## The River

**System:** Stanton
**Location:** MicroTech
**Coordinates:** `Near Port Tressler area`

### Description
A flowing river canyon cutting through the frozen landscape of MicroTech, one of the few liquid water features visible.

### How to Reach
Land near Port Tressler and traverse the frozen canyons

### What Makes It Special
Rare liquid water on a frozen moon, dramatic canyon landscapes

### Lore & Background
Geological anomaly bringing liquid water to the surface

### Visitor Tips
Watch your speed in the canyon. Great opportunity for glider flights.

## MicroTech Christmas Tree

**System:** Stanton
**Location:** MicroTech
**Coordinates:** `Near Orison`

### Description
A conical mountain formation that resembles a decorated Christmas tree when viewed from above.

### How to Reach
Fly over the mountains near Orison and look down

### What Makes It Special
Natural rock formation creating holiday imagery, great for photos

### Lore & Background
Purely geological coincidence, though locals embrace it as a seasonal landmark

### Visitor Tips
Best viewed during the holiday season in-game. Higher altitude recommended.
