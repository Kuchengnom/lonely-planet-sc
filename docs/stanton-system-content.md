## Overview

The Stanton system is a single star system, and one of two fully colonized systems in the UEE. It is the only system with a green zone reaching four planets, making it suitable for human life. All worlds have undergone limited terraforming and have flora and fauna with divergent ecologies. They are owned by corporate entities within the United Empire of Earth.

**System Classification:** Single Star | 4 Planets | 12 Moons | 2 Asteroid Belts | 24 Stations

## Planets

### Stanton I: Hurston
**Type:** Super-Earth | **Owned by:** Hurston Dynamics
> A wealth of ore and other resources are mined here to manufacture Hurston Dynamics' line of munitions and weapons. Heavy industry has resulted in severe pollution across the planet, devastating the ecosystem.

**Landing Zone:** Lorville — The planetary capital and primary spaceport, existing in the shadow of the Central corporate headquarters.
**Atmosphere:** Breathable but heavily polluted. Hazy daytime conditions with dense particulates.
**Biomes:** Wasteland, strip mine, trash mesa, savannah, acidic, polluted coast, bombardment craters.
**Notable:** The planet's savanna biome has been devastated by industry. Air quality is a concern for residents.
**Moons (4):** Arial, Aberdeen, Magda, Ita

---

### Stanton II: Crusader
**Type:** Low-mass Gas Giant | **Owned by:** Crusader Industries
> A low mass gas giant that features a breathable atmosphere at high altitudes. This was an ideal situation for ship manufacturer Crusader Industries, who now occupies the planet's floating platforms to build massive ships like the Starliner.

**Landing Zone:** Orison — A city in the clouds, offering breathtaking views of Crusader's commercial shipbuilding platforms.
**Atmosphere:** Breathable at high altitudes (80km). Low gravity and suit-less working atmosphere.
**Notable:** The shipyards are eerily beautiful, with huge transport ships suspended in mid-atmosphere surrounded by a webbing of Crusader facilities.
**Moons (3):** Cellin, Daymar, Yela

---

### Stanton III: ArcCorp
**Type:** Super-Earth | **Owned by:** ArcCorp
> Buildings cover a majority of the planet's surface with residential areas relegated to the poles while the company's factories, business headquarters, financial and commercial districts are located in between.

**Landing Zone:** Area18 — One of the main commercial ports for interplanetary traffic, site of the ArcCorp Tower.
**Atmosphere:** Engineered to Earth specifications.
**Notable:** The single most industrialized world in Human space. Layer after layer of factory; new facilities are built on top of existing ones. Home to the ArcCorp Tower.
**Moons (2):** Lyria, Wala

---

### Stanton IV: microTech
**Type:** Terraformed Planet | **Owned by:** microTech
> A terraforming error left the planet with an unnaturally dense cloud cover and a colder than average climate, making it an ideal location for its owner to house its heat-sensitive computing and manufacturing centers.

**Landing Zone:** New Babbage — The primary city, built from permafrost by the microTech corporation.
**Atmosphere:** Cold, sub-arctic. Frequent blizzards at high latitudes.
**Notable:** Known for rivers that shouldn't flow, forests that refused to freeze, and caves that glow in the dark. The silence between ice storms is worth the flight alone.
**Already documented in:** `src/pages/stanton/microtech/index.mdx`

## Asteroid Formations

### Aaron Halo
Located between the orbits of Crusader and ArcCorp. The system's primary asteroid belt. It is a major location for mining operations and has Rest Stop stations at some of its Lagrangian points.

### 2930-CRU983
A named asteroid in the Stanton system.

## Content Strategy Notes

- **Hurston** offers the strongest contrast to microTech: polluted, hot, industrially devastated rather than cold and pristine. The savannah and acidic biomes provide interesting exploration opportunities.
- **Crusader** is unique as a gas giant with a breathable atmosphere. Orison is a visually distinct cloud city. The moons (Cellin, Daymar, Yela) are very diverse: volcanic, desert, and icy respectively.
- **ArcCorp** is the most urban location. It is visually dense. Area18 and the ArcCorp Tower are the primary draws. Lyria's cryovolcanoes offer a unique natural contrast.
