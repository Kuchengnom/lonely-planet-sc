# Pyro System — Content Catalogue

> Compiled for Lonely Planet: Star Citizen. Pyro is the frontier's frontier — lawless, volcanic, and beautiful in the way that makes you check your insurance policy.

---

## Overview

The Pyro system is a single star system on the frontier of UEE space. It is characterized by extreme volcanic activity, unstable geology, and a near-total absence of centralized law enforcement. The system is effectively ungoverned — while the UEE claims sovereignty, their presence is minimal, and most settlements operate under local rule, corporate contract, or simple anarchy.

**System Classification:** Single Star | 6 Planets | Multiple Moons | Volcanic Activity

---

## Planets

### Pyro I
**Type**: Terrestrial (Volcanic) | **Status**: Unclaimed / Lawless
> The innermost planet. A hellscape of magma, ash, and sulfur. The surface temperature is high enough to melt standard EVA suits on contact. And yet, it is home to some of the most profitable mining operations in the frontier.

**Notable Features:**
- Surface is predominantly molten or recently-solidified lava
- Atmosphere is toxic and corrosive
- Rich in rare minerals and volcanic compounds
- Extreme mining operations with limited infrastructure
- No formal landing zones — landing is at your own risk

---

### Pyro II
**Type**: Terrestrial | **Status**: Unclaimed
> Less volcanic than Pyro I but still geologically active. The surface is scarred with ancient lava flows and active magma vents.

---

### Pyro III
**Type**: Terrestrial | **Status**: Unclaimed
> A temperate-ish world by Pyro standards, meaning you might survive on the surface for more than five minutes without a pressure suit.

---

### Pyro IV
**Type**: Terrestrial | **Status**: Unclaimed
> Mid-system planet with varied terrain. Rumors of hidden settlements and pirate bases.

---

### Pyro V
**Type**: Terrestrial | **Status**: Unclaimed
> Outer terrestrial world. Cold by Pyro standards, which is still warmer than you'd like.

---

### Pyro VI
**Type**: Gas Giant | **Status**: Unclaimed
> The system's outer gas giant. Unlike Crusader, there is no breathable atmosphere. Massive, turbulent, and home to some of the most spectacular storms in the known systems.

---

## Asteroid Fields

### The Pyro Belt
The system's primary asteroid field, located between the inner and outer planets. Rich in minerals due to constant volcanic ejecta. Also rich in pirates for the same reason.

## Key Themes for Guides

- **Lawlessness**: The UEE barely enforces law here. Self-defense is not just a right, it's a necessity.
- **Volcanic Activity**: Hot, dangerous, unpredictable. The geology is as hostile as the locals.
- **Mining**: The reason anyone comes here. Volcanic minerals are valuable, and the desperate come to extract them.
- **Pirate Activity**: Pyro is a haven for outlaws, smugglers, and those who've made the UEE's wanted list.
- **Frontier Spirit**: The people who live here are a breed apart. Rugged, self-reliant, and armed.

---

## Content Strategy Notes

- **Pyro I** should emphasize the extreme danger and extreme reward. This is a planet that actively tries to kill you and the only people who come here are either crazy or desperate. Or both.
- **Ruin Station** is the system's "capital" in the loosest possible sense. It's chaotic, dangerous, and fascinating.
- **The Pyro Belt** is the Wild West of space — mining claims, pirate raids, and the occasional claim-jumping shootout.
- **General tone**: More gritty, more dangerous, more "frontier justice" than Stanton. Think Deadwood in space.
