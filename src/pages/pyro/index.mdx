## About This Guide

This edition covers the Pyro System — six planets of varying hostility, a rogue's gallery of moons, and more ways to die than any sane traveler should encounter. The information herein is compiled from UEE surveys, independent scouting reports, and the occasional properly-monitored distress transmission.
