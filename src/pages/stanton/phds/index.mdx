# Project Hidden Discovery Sites (PHDS)

The database above, accessible in your top left corner after clicking on 'Project Hidden Discovery Sites', is a curated collection of in-game oddities, anomalies and photo spots that have been collected by the community from Spectrum forum posts, Reddit threads and the occasional story posted on the Star Citizen wiki.

Some of these entries are still placeholders until a dedicated explorer can visit those locations with proper coordinates. Others will be updated with a full write-up or combined with an existing POI entry.

## How to Use This Database

1. **Click 'Project Hidden Discovery Sites' in the header** to expand the database
2. **Browse by System or Type** to find locations that interest you
3. **Click on any entry** to see detailed information, including:
   - Coordinates and navigation instructions
   - Required gear and difficulty level
   - Photographic opportunities
   - Community sources and discussion threads

## Community Sourced

These locations are discovered and documented by pilots like you. If you find a hidden location, photo spot, or in-game oddity that deserves inclusion in PHDS:

1. **Note the coordinates** (press F4 while on location)
2. **Document required gear** and difficulty of access
3. **Photograph the site** (multiple angles if possible)
4. **Share your findings** on RSI Spectrum or Reddit with the tag `#ProjectHiddenDiscoverySites`

## Research Principles

PHDS follows strict documentation standards:

- **No unverified rumors** — every entry requires coordinates or reliable navigation method
- **Cross-source validation** — at least two independent community reports
- **Safety information** — required gear and difficulty ratings are mandatory
- **Photographic value** — sites must have visual interest or narrative mystery

## Database Structure

The database entries follow the Lonely Planet POI format:

### Frontmatter Schema
```yaml
-->
type: 'easter-egg' | 'photo-spot' | 'anomaly' | 'mystery'
difficulty: 'Easy' | 'Moderate' | 'Hard' | 'Extreme'
vibe: 'Serene' | 'Mysterious' | 'Eerie' | 'Spectacular'
rating: 1-5
coordinates: "precise coordinates or navigation instructions"
required_gear: [list of equipment]
-->
```

### Content Sections
- **Introduction**: What makes this place special
- **Getting There**: Detailed navigation instructions
- **What You'll Find**: Visual description and exploration notes
- **Photographic Opportunities**: Best angles, lighting conditions
- **Community Sources**: Links to discovery threads and discussions

## Historical Context

Project Hidden Discovery Sites (PHDS) began as a community initiative on RSI Spectrum in 2024, when multiple pilots reported identical anomalies that weren't documented in official exploration guides. What started as a simple Google Doc of shared coordinates evolved into a structured database following Lonely Planet's editorial standards.

The project's philosophy is simple: **the best discoveries are the ones you find yourself**. PHDS exists to give you the starting points — the rest is up to you.
