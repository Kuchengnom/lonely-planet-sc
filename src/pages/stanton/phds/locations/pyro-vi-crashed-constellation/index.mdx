## What You'll Find

The crashed Constellation Phoenix is a medium-sized luxury transport, now partially buried in Pyro VI's landscape.

### The Main Hull

The primary fuselage is remarkably intact, suggesting the wreck occurred from a power-off landing rather than destructive crash. Constellations have been noted for robust hulls, and this example exemplifies that design. The phoenix symbol on the side is still visible through volcanic ash.

The cockpit is empty; the evacuation hatch was opened from the inside. Data recovered from flight recorders (说法不一, some from data miners, others from lore updates) indicate the crew evacuated via emergency eject pods, now missing — likely carried away by volcanic activity or removed by scavengers.
