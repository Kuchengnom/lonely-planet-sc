### Navigation data

**The First River** — `ships-on-lake.jpeg`
- New Babbage → heading **205°** → quantum to **MT DataCenter E2Q-NSG-Y**, cancel at ~**900 km** remaining
- Alt: fly heading 205° manually for ~440 km
- Visual cue: thawed valley, darker ground, open water in the snowfield

**The Canyon River** — `canyon-river-aerial.jpeg`
- **MT DataCenter KH3-AAE-L** → heading **027°** → ~440 km
- Alt 1: midpoint between **OM-6** and **OM-3**
- Alt 2: **Dunboro** → heading **265°** → ~60 km
- Visual cue: cut canyon walls, autumn forest, deeper colour palette

**The Frozen Lakes** — `ice-arch.jpeg`
- Sub-polar latitudes; no fixed nav target — search the flat white expanses
- Visual cue: ice arch and column formations in shallower lake regions

**The Bioluminescent Caves** — `bioluminescent-cave.jpeg`
- No official map markers — find by low-altitude tundra flying
- Visual cue: dark openings in cliff faces; cave ceiling covered in blue-cyan glow

**Surface Outposts** — `surface-outpost.jpeg`
- **Dunboro**: from MT DataCenter KH3-AAE-L, heading **265°**, ~60 km
- Unnamed crash sites throughout the tundra with no nav markers
